Our Plans

Plans that suits everybody !

  • Basic Plan

    Our Free plan is designed for those new to fitness or individuals seeking flexibility without the commitment of a monthly fee. It provides limited access to our gym facilities and equipment during off-peak hours, allowing you to experience the basics of a fitness routine. This plan is an excellent choice for people who prefer a no-cost option to stay active, offering an introductory glimpse into the gym environment. Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or looking for a low-key way to maintain your health, our Free plan supports your wellness goals without any financial investment.

    Join Now

  • Premium Plan

    The Premium plan is tailored for fitness enthusiasts who desire comprehensive access to all gym amenities and services. This plan includes unrestricted access to the gym, all group fitness classes, and a wide range of equipment at any time. Members also benefit from personalized workout programs designed by our certified trainers to meet individual goals, whether it's weight loss, muscle gain, or improved athletic performance. The Premium plan is perfect for those who are serious about their fitness journey and want the flexibility to work out on their own schedule, with the added benefit of professional guidance to ensure they achieve their health and fitness goals.

    Join Now

  • VIP Plan

    Our VIP membership is the epitome of luxury fitness, offering an unparalleled experience for those who demand the best. In addition to all the benefits of the Premium plan, VIP members enjoy exclusive access to our spa and wellness area, private training sessions with our elite personal trainers, and priority booking for classes and facilities. This plan also includes special wellness consultations and nutrition planning to fully support your holistic health. The VIP plan is designed for individuals seeking a personalized, high-end fitness experience that goes beyond traditional gym access, providing all the tools and services needed for optimal health and wellness.
